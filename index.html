Tournament Flow

How it works

From signup to prize pool in six steps. Everything is automated.

Register

Click the Register button on the tournament announcement. Enter your Riot ID and you're signed up.

Account Verified

Your League account is verified through the Riot API to ensure fair and competitive matchups for everyone.

Check In

Before the tournament starts, you'll get a notification to confirm you're ready. Quick and easy.

Teams Balanced

Teams are balanced by estimated MMR so every match is competitive. Choose your preferred role and we'll place you there.

Play Matches

Double elimination means everyone gets a second chance. Live brackets update after every match so you can follow along.

Win Prizes

Top teams take home the prize pool. Both free and paid tournaments available.
