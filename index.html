What Raven Does

Built for gaming communities

A full suite of tools so your server doesn't need a dozen different bots.

🎵

Music Playback

Queue songs from YouTube, skip, pause, and control playback — all from Discord slash commands.

🪙

Economy System

A server currency with games, a shop, gambling, and a dungeon roguelike. Coins have no real monetary value.

🏆

LoL Tournaments

Fully-structured 5v5 League of Legends tournaments with skill-based team balancing and visual brackets.

🛡️

Moderation

Purge, kick, ban, timeout, and lock channels. Role-hierarchy aware with full audit log support.

📺

Media Requests

Members can request movies and TV shows directly via Radarr and Sonarr integration.

🃏

Card System

Collect, trade, and battle with cards. A full card pack economy with PvP battles and XP progression.

League of Legends

Community tournament platform

Raven handles the full lifecycle of a community 5v5 tournament — from registration through bracket and results.

✍️

Registration & Check-In

Players register with their Riot ID. Account verification is handled automatically via the Riot API. A check-in system confirms attendance before the tournament starts.

⚖️

Skill-Based Team Balancing

Teams are balanced using players' ranked data pulled from Riot's API, ensuring fair and competitive matchups across all participating teams.

🗺️

Visual Brackets

Automatically generated bracket images update in real time as matches are reported. Supports double-elimination and single-elimination formats.

🎮

Role Assignment

Players indicate their preferred roles (Top, Jungle, Mid, ADC, Support). The bot assigns roles across teams to maximize player satisfaction.

👥

Substitute Pool

No-shows at check-in are automatically moved to a sub pool. If a player drops mid-tournament, subs are offered their spot instantly.

📋

Live Dashboard

A pinned dashboard message shows real-time tournament state — registered players, team assignments, match results, and standings.
